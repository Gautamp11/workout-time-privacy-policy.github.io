Privacy Policy for Workout Time

Workout Time is built and maintained by asyncawait as a free application. This app is provided as-is and is intended for personal fitness tracking.

Information Collection and Use

Workout Time does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. All workout and exercise data is stored locally on the user’s device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Workout Time does not use third-party services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

Workout Time does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

All data remains on the user’s device. Users have full control over their information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
